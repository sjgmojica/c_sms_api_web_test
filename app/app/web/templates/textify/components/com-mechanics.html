Mechanics

  1. Textify is open to individuals or teams with 2 - 5 members. Get started by creating your free Chikka API account at api.chikka.com.

  2. Go to textifyph.eventbrite.com to register for free. Don’t forget to submit your team name, your team members, app details and other important information.

  3. To officially qualify, submit your app details at hackathon.io/textify.

  4. Remember, all entries must be submitted online by November 03, 2014 together with a short app description (150 words or less) and a link to a two-minute video demo.

  5. Then on November 10, 2014, we’ll reveal the Top 10 teams to join us for the finals.

  6. If you are part of the Top 10, we we will see you on November 22, 2014 for the Live Pitch and Q&A!
